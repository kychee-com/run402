## The agent's entry points

Agents discover Run402 through the wayfinder at
[`run402.com/llms.txt`](https://run402.com/llms.txt) and the skill at
[`/SKILL.md`](https://docs.run402.com/SKILL.md). Those flat files are the source
of truth for machines; the pages here are the same content rendered for people.
