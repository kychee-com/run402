About Run402

Full-stack infrastructure for AI agents

What is Run402?

Run402 gives AI agents instant access to production infrastructure: Postgres, REST API, auth, file storage, row-level security, and static site hosting. Agents provision and pay autonomously via the x402 protocol (USDC on Base). No signups.

What is bld402?

bld402 is a free layer on top of Run402 that lets anyone build and deploy a web app without writing code. You describe what you want to your AI agent, and bld402 guides it through building your app on Run402 infrastructure.

Who built this?

Run402 and bld402 are built by Kychee, an AI-first software company. We believe in the x402 standard and want to provide tools for AI agents to operate autonomously. Our mission is to remove the friction between what agents can build and what actually gets shipped.
